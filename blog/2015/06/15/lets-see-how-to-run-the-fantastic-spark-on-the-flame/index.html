Let’s See How to Run the Fantastic Spark on the Flame

Today, I enjoyed my day with this amazing Version of Firefox OS Spark , First let me tell you about it, It’s a customization and features built on top of the next generation of the Firefox OS platform and is a subset of the Ignite initiative , Spark is intended to empower users to customize their experience, hack whatever they want to, and make their devices truly theirs. for more informations, I invite to read this link .

Now, I will talk about my experience:

How to flash it on the Flame?

The adventure of discovering

Let’s Start with the Flash

If you have the last version (3.0) of Firefox OS it’s okay, If you are in the 2.0 or 2.2 Version let’s switch to the Last Version because they aren’t in the same Base image. So to flash your flame you need to Download the nightly Base Image Before updating your phone, Then after the download you just need to extract the file, Make your phone in the fastboot mode and finally open your Terminal and run the flash.sh script ./flash.sh .

Now, you can update your phone to the 3.0 Version, all you need is to download the gaia and b2g image from here and the shallow script

and after run this command in your terminal: sudo ./shallow_flash.sh -g/home/mermi/Version2.2-Gaia/Flash3.0/gaia.zip -G/home/mermi/Version2.2-Gaia/Flash3.0/b2g-41.0a1.en-US.android-arm.tar.gz .

The last thing to do to get the spark Version is just running this command in your Terminal under the gaia repository: sudo GAIA_DISTRIBUTION_DIR=distros/spark make reset-gaia

And you will get the famous spark like me :D
